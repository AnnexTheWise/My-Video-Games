Main character of Persona 5

This is Joker, main protagonist of Persona 5. He is a falsely accused high school student trying to save the world from corrupted people trying to make the world in their image. He does that by exploring through what is called the metaverse. While in the metaverse, he and his team have unique abilities known as Personas. They use these personas to fight and save the world from the corrupted society.

Main character of Undertale
